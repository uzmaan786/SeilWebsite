SenseIn Lab - IIT Palakkad

About Us

The Sensors and Electronic Instrumentation Laboratory at IIT Palakkad is dedicated to sensor development, instrumentation, and measurement research. The lab designs and develops various application-oriented sensors and signal conditioning circuits. It is part of the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Palakkad, Kerala, India.
