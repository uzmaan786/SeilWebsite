SenseIn Lab - IIT Palakkad

About Us

The Sensors and Electronic Instrumentation Laboratory at IIT Palakkad is dedicated to sensor development, instrumentation, and measurement research. The lab designs and develops various application-oriented sensors and signal conditioning circuits. It is part of the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Palakkad, Kerala, India.

Our Vision

Our vision is to drive innovation in sensor development and measurement through impactful research that addresses the needs of society and industry. We aim to become leaders in sensor technology by fostering expertise and advancing knowledge in this dynamic field.

Our Mission

Our mission is to design and develop advanced sensors, signal conditioning circuits, and instrumentation techniques that deliver accurate measurements in an efficient manner. We are committed to supporting industries in the advancement of sensor technology, instrumentation, and measurement systems to drive innovation and efficiency.
